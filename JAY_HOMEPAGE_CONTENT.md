# Product Overview

## What is Jay?

Jay is your personal AI moving assistant from Just Move In. Think of Jay as a knowledgeable friend who has helped thousands of people move home and knows exactly what needs to be done, when, and how.

### The Problem Jay Solves

Moving home is one of life's most stressful experiences. With an average of 40+ tasks to complete, multiple deadlines to track, and dozens of service providers to contact, it's easy to feel overwhelmed. Traditional moving support means:
- Waiting on hold for call centres
- Filling out endless forms
- Missing critical deadlines
- Forgotten tasks discovered too late
- No personalised guidance

### The Jay Solution

Jay provides:
- **24/7 Availability** - Get help whenever you need it, day or night
- **Personalised Guidance** - Tailored advice based on your specific move
- **Proactive Reminders** - Never miss an important deadline
- **Multi-Channel Access** - Chat via web, WhatsApp, or voice call
- **Comprehensive Knowledge** - Expert information on every aspect of moving

# How Jay Works

## The Multi-Agent Architecture

Jay isn't a single AI - it's an orchestrated team of specialised agents working together:

### Main Agent (Jay)
The primary conversational interface you interact with. Jay:
- Understands your questions and needs
- Coordinates with specialist agents
- Maintains conversation context
- Delivers personalised responses

### Specialist Agents

| Agent | Specialisation |
|-------|----------------|
| **Checklist Agent** | Creates, updates, and manages your moving checklist with smart task categorisation |
| **Appointment Agent** | Handles all scheduling operations with availability checking |
| **Location Agent** | Provides weather, neighbourhood data, and local amenities information |
| **Insurance Agent** | Delivers comprehensive insurance guidance and options |
| **Validation Agent** | Ensures contact information is correctly formatted |
| **Reminder Agent** | Manages WhatsApp scheduled message reminders |

## The Conversation Flow

```
User Question
     ↓
Main Agent (Jay) understands intent
     ↓
Specialist agent consulted if needed
     ↓
Information retrieved from knowledge base
     ↓
Response personalised to user context
     ↓
Delivered via user's chosen channel
```

# FAQs

## General Questions

### What is Jay?
Jay is Just Move In's AI-powered moving assistant. It helps UK residents manage every aspect of their property move through intelligent conversation across web, WhatsApp, and voice channels.

### Is Jay free to use?
Jay is included as part of the Just Move In service. Contact JMI for specific pricing and package details.

### What hours is Jay available?
Jay is available 24/7, every day of the year. You can get moving assistance whenever you need it.

### What languages does Jay speak?
Jay supports 10 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Hindi, Russian, Portuguese, Japanese, Italian, and Dutch.

## Capabilities

### Can Jay set up my utilities?
Jay provides comprehensive guidance on utility setup but cannot directly activate services on your behalf. For energy and some other services, JMI's phone team can assist with actual setup.

### Can Jay book property viewings?
No, Jay cannot book apartment or property viewings. Jay focuses on move assistance after you've secured your new home.

### Can Jay remember what I told it?
Yes! Jay has a sophisticated memory system that extracts and stores relevant facts from your conversations. You won't need to repeat information across sessions.

### Can I upload documents?
Yes! You can upload PDFs, images, and documents. Jay will analyse them and can answer questions about their contents (like your EPC rating or lease terms).

## Privacy & Security

### Is my data safe with Jay?
Yes. All conversations and documents are encrypted, stored securely, and protected by enterprise-grade security measures.

### Can I delete my data?
Yes. You can request complete account and data deletion through our customer service team.

### Does Jay share my information?
No. Your personal information is never shared with other users. Jay maintains strict privacy boundaries.

## Technical Questions

### What browsers support Jay?
Jay works in all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

### Do I need to install anything?
No installation required for web chat. WhatsApp works through your existing WhatsApp account.

### What if Jay can't answer my question?
Jay will acknowledge when it doesn't have information and suggest alternatives, such as contacting JMI's customer service team or searching for current information online.
